# External Sources Manifest

This folder stores official external sources for reviewer-response workstreams tied to the JOLE Franco school reform project.

Core sources included in the first ingestion pass:

- `BOE` legal texts for `Law 56/1961`, `Decreto 258/1962`, and `Decreto 2310/1970`.
- `INE Censo 1981` tables on migration, activity, occupation, sector composition, and fertility.
- `Banco de Espana` working paper `dt9409` documenting reconstructed historical employment series from the EPA.

INE table codes in scope:

- `01024`: activity status by territory and sex
- `01025`: activity rate by territory and sex
- `01029`: employed population by branch and age group
- `01030`: employed population by sex, sector and age group
- `01033`: employed population by sex, profession and branch
- `01057`: migration relation 1970-1981 with active population
- `01058`: migration relation 1970-1981 with employed-unemployed
- `01059`: migration relation 1970-1981 with branch of activity
- `01061`: province of residence in 1970 vs 1981
- `01064`: children ever born by age group
- `01065`: children ever born by year of birth
- `01072`: children ever born in first marriages by activity, marriage year and age
