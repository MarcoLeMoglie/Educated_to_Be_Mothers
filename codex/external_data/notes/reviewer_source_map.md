# Reviewer Source Map

## Law 56/1961

- `processed/boe_legal_timeline.csv`
  - pins down the legal timing of `Law 56/1961`, `Decreto 258/1962`, and `Decreto 2310/1970`
  - useful for alternative exposure codings: `1961`, `1962`, and `1970`

- `processed/ine_01024_clean.dta`
  - broad 1981 activity status by territory and sex
  - useful for showing how female activity relates to geography in a post-law cross-section

- `processed/ine_01025_clean.dta`
  - activity rates by territory and sex
  - useful for simple descriptive validation of female labor-force attachment

- `processed/ine_01072_clean.dta`
  - completed fertility in first marriages by activity
  - useful for the mechanism test `employment/activity -> fertility`

## 1973 crisis

- `notes/bde_dt9409_extract.txt`
  - searchable extract from the Banco de Espana historical employment reconstruction
  - useful as source support for the pre-1976 labor-market series and sector definitions

- `processed/ine_01029_clean.dta`
  - employment by branch and age group
  - useful for building sector exposure benchmarks

- `processed/ine_01030_clean.dta`
  - employment by sex, sector and age group
  - useful for female sector concentration in agriculture, industry, construction and services

- `processed/ine_01033_clean.dta`
  - employment by sex, profession and branch
  - useful for a richer mapping from sector to occupational composition

## Internal migration

- `processed/ine_01057_clean.dta`
  - active population by sex, migration status `1970-1981`, and age group
  - useful for placebo tests on female labor-force composition among movers/stayers

- `processed/ine_01058_clean.dta`
  - employed/unemployed by sex, migration status `1970-1981`, and age group
  - useful for distinguishing migration from labor-market sorting

- `processed/ine_01059_clean.dta`
  - branch of activity by sex and migration status `1970-1981`
  - useful for asking whether movers re-sort toward specific sectors

- `processed/ine_01061_clean.dta`
  - province of residence in `1970` versus `1981`
  - useful for direct migration-flow matrices

## Fertility and cohorts

- `processed/ine_01064_clean.dta`
  - children ever born by age group
  - useful for broad fertility composition checks

- `processed/ine_01065_clean.dta`
  - children ever born by year of birth
  - useful for post-1950 cohort extensions and monotonic-trend checks
