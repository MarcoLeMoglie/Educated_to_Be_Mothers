Message: The footnote states that, because the belief data come from a single 1990 survey wave, “standard errors cannot be clustered at the year-by-year-of-birth level.”  Technically, clustering by birth cohort remains feasible in a cross-section—even though the two-way cohort × survey-year scheme used elsewhere degenerates to one dimension.
